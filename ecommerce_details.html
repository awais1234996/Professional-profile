  • Category: Ecommerce Web Application

Admin Panel Features

The Admin Panel is the control center for your eCommerce platform, giving you full power to manage products, sales, users, and system behavior.

  • Categories & Subcategories: Organize your products into main categories and nested subcategories to help users browse easily.
  • Suppliers: Manage product suppliers with full details like contact info, delivery terms, and purchase history.
  • Quantity & Inventory Management: Update stock in real time. Low-stock alerts help prevent overselling.
  • Product Management: Add products with single or multiple variations like size, color, or package types. Upload images, set prices, and discounts.
  • Orders: View, update, and process customer orders. Change status (Pending, Processing, Shipped, Delivered), generate invoices, and track payments.
  • POS (Point of Sale): A real-time selling interface for in-store use. Add products directly to cart, apply discounts, scan barcodes, accept different payment types (cash, card, UPI), and print invoices instantly. Ideal for retail stores integrated with your web stock.
  • Contact: View and respond to customer inquiries submitted via the contact form. Manage customer support.
  • Sales Information: Access real-time sales reports, revenue analytics, and daily/monthly performance charts. Useful for business decisions and forecasting.
  • Create Users: Add new Admins, Staff, or Agents with specific access privileges.
  • Role & Permission Management: Define user roles (e.g., Admin, Manager, Sales Staff, Support). Assign permissions to each role such as:
    • Full access to Products but no access to Sales Reports
    • POS-only role for cashiers
    • Support role that can only access Contact messages and Orders
    This ensures your team can only access the areas relevant to their job.

User Side Features

The User Side is optimized for customer satisfaction, with intuitive navigation and smooth checkout flows.

  • Home Page: Highlights best-selling and latest products with banners and deals.
  • About Page: Tells your brand story, your mission, and what makes your business special.
  • Product Page: Detailed product view with images, pricing, specifications, stock availability, and variation selectors (e.g., size or color).
  • Category Page: Filters and displays products based on the selected category/subcategory.
  • Order Page: Lists user orders with order ID, total amount, status, and payment info.
  • Checkout Page: Secure checkout where users confirm billing/shipping info, choose payment method, and place orders.
  • User Order Information Page: Shows specific order details, shipment tracking, and support messages.
  • User Invoice Detail Page: Auto-generated invoice that users can view, download, or print. Includes order summary, tax, and billing info.
