Admin Panel Features

The Admin Panel for the News Management System gives full backend control over content publishing, event organization, and user engagement. It’s designed to help administrators manage media-rich content, organize events, and interact with the audience.

  • Categories & Subcategories: Organize news and events into logical sections like "Politics", "Sports", or "Technology", with nested subcategories like "Football", "Elections", etc.
  • News: Add, update, and delete news articles. Each news item includes a title, image, rich text content, tags, category, and publish date. Admins can schedule publishing or mark items as drafts.
  • Trending News: Highlight the top-performing or hand-picked news articles that appear in a special "Trending" section on the user side. These may be selected manually or based on views/engagement.
  • Event Management: Create and manage events (e.g., conferences, political rallies, webinars). Include event title, banner, description, date, time, location (physical or virtual), and registration links if applicable.
  • Poll Management: Create public opinion polls to boost engagement. Admins can add questions with multiple options, control vote limits, and view poll results analytics.
  • Contact Management: Receive and manage contact messages or inquiries submitted by users. Respond to feedback or route issues to the right team.
  • Live Video Management: Integrate YouTube or other streaming platforms by embedding live video links. Admins can schedule when the live section goes live, and replace streams when necessary.
  • Logo Management: Upload or update the main site logo and favicon via the admin panel. Useful for branding or seasonal updates (e.g., dark mode logo).

User Side Features

The User Side is built for readers, attendees, and participants to explore news, engage in events, and stay updated with live content. It's designed for ease of navigation and high engagement.

  • Home Page: Displays a mix of latest news, featured articles, live video section, trending news, and upcoming events—all in a visually engaging layout.
  • About Page: Tells users what your platform is about—its vision, team, history, or mission. This builds trust and connection with your audience.
  • Event Page: A dynamic page listing all active or upcoming events with filters by date or category. Users can view event details and click to register or join live sessions.
  • Category Page: Lists all news articles under a selected category or subcategory. Useful for readers to dive deeper into specific topics of interest.
  • Latest News Section: Continuously updated section displaying the newest articles or announcements, keeping users informed in real-time.
  • Image Gallery: A grid-based gallery for event photos, behind-the-scenes images, or thematic visual content to enhance engagement.
  • Survey Module: Allows users to participate in polls and surveys to express opinions or feedback on various topics.
  • Video Gallery: Organized display of recorded event videos, interviews, and other multimedia content for on-demand viewing.
